Our Responsibility

Ensuring the legitimacy of respondents is crucial because data quality is a paramount concern for businesses involved in market research. To guarantee study quality, we employ a comprehensive validation strategy known as Cred Encuesta Quality Suites.

IP confirmation

During recruitment, we verify the IP address of new panelists to confirm their location within the market area. This process involves checking against a known list of fraudulent servers to identify any potential fraud.

Prevention of Duplication

Furthermore, it is essential to prevent the same individual from participating in the same survey multiple times. This applies whether they come from different panels used for the project or have multiple instances within the same panel.

Loyalty Monitor

Preventing fraudulent respondents from participating in surveys has been successfully demonstrated, regardless of the sources, markets, or audiences. This practice is standard for all consumer research with a response rate (IR) exceeding 10%.

Unique Respondents to Surveys

At the outset of a study, our approach effectively reduces duplicate responses. By utilizing proprietary and industry-accepted digital fingerprinting technology, we ensure that only unique respondents participate in the research.
